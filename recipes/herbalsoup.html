Herbal Soup

Description:

Good for cold days or for when you're feeling weak.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Chicken Legs
  • Dried Scallops
  • Female Ginseng
  • Yam Root
  • Goji Berries
  • Red Dates
  • Salt
  • White Pepper
  • 2 liters of Water

Steps:

  1. Broil chicken legs for 5 minutes to get rid of dirt
  2. Add all ingredients into a big pot
  3. Add 2 liters of water
  4. Bring to a rapid boil for 30 minutes
  5. Simmer soup for a minimum of 2 hours, ideally an entire afternoon
  6. Taste and adjust with seasonings
