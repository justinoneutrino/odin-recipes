Salmon Don

Description:

A nutritious weekday meal for something fast and easy.

Ingredients:

  • Salmon
  • Avocado
  • Rice
  • Light Soy Sauce
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps:

  1. Season salmon with salt and black pepper
  2. Pan fry salmon till desired doneness
  3. Mash avocado and mix with rice with light soy sauce and dark soy sauce
  4. Serve with salmon on top
