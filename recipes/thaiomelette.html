Thai Omelette

Description:

Comfort food at its finest. A weeknight dinner classic.

Ingredients:

  • 3 Eggs
  • Minced Pork
  • Spring Onions
  • Shallots
  • Olive Oil
  • Fish Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • White Pepper

Steps:

  1. Combine all ingredients and whisk it until mixture is foamy and full of airbubbles
  2. Add olive oil to frying pan and heat it until you see smoke
  3. Add mixture to pan and spread evenly ensuring that edges start crisping up
  4. Wait till omelette has set and is evenly browned on the other side
  5. Flip the omelette to brown the other side
  6. Remove from frying pan and serve hot
